The Spark

Why Engineering?

What drew me to software engineering was an innate drive to build — to move from concept to functioning product entirely through code. The ability to architect systems from the ground up, exercise precise control over every interaction, and shape exactly how users experience a product felt less like a career choice and more like a creative imperative. Engineering isn't just what I study; it's how I think.

The Sweet Spot

Where I Thrive

I'm most energized at the precise intersection of logic and lived experience — fine-tuning JavaScript validation flows, engineering nuanced UX state transitions, and choreographing CSS animations that feel intentional rather than decorative.
